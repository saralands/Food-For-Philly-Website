Food for Philly We are a comprehensive and accessible resource for the hungry and poor in Philadelphia. We aim to engage with the city's citizenry and government to help end the fight for hunger in the City of Brotherly Love. Founded in 2015 out of the 14th floor of 'The Hub' on 17th and Chestnut, Food for Philly offers the hungry and poor in Philadelphia short-term aid and long-term resources and assistance.
